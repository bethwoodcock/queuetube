## Name
QueueTube

## Short description (up to 132 chars)
Queue your YouTube subscription feed and watch videos back-to-back like a playlist — with skip, prev, and autoplay.

## Detailed description
Tired of clicking each video one by one in your YouTube subscriptions?

QueueTube adds a single "Queue Subscriptions" button to your YouTube subscriptions feed. Click it and every video on the page is instantly queued for back-to-back playback — just like a playlist.

**Features:**
• One-click queuing of your entire subscription feed
• Automatically plays the next video when one ends
• Skip forward or back with prev/next buttons
• Click any video in the queue panel to jump straight to it
• Queue panel shows real video titles so you know what's coming next
• Scroll down before queuing to load even more videos (300+ possible)
• Minimal and unobtrusive — only appears on your subscriptions page

**Privacy:**
QueueTube stores your queue locally on your device only. No data is ever collected or transmitted anywhere.
