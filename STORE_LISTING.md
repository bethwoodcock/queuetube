# Chrome Web Store Listing Copy

## Name
QueueTube

## Short description (up to 132 chars)
Queue your YouTube subscription feed and watch videos back-to-back like a playlist — with autoplay, skip, and progress bars.

## Detailed description
Tired of clicking each video one by one in your subscriptions? QueueTube adds a single button to your YouTube subscriptions page that instantly queues everything up and autoplays them in order, with full skip and navigation controls.

Features:
▶️ One-click queuing - hit the button and your subscriptions start playing immediately
⏭️ Autoplay - when a video ends, the next one starts automatically
⏮️ Skip controls - jump forward or back in your queue at any time
📋 Queue panel - shows all queued videos with titles so you know what's coming next
🔴 Watch progress bars - videos you've partially watched show a red progress bar in the queue panel, just like YouTube's own thumbnails, so you can decide whether to skip
🖱️ Click to jump - click any video in the panel to skip straight to it
📜 Load more with scrolling - scroll down your subscriptions before hitting Queue to load even more videos (you can queue 300+ this way!)
🔀 Guilt-free sidebar browsing -- spot something interesting in the sidebar? Click it and watch it normally. Your queue is still saved, so just hit play in the panel to jump straight back to where you were. Or open it in a new tab to keep your queue playing uninterrupted in the original tab

New in v1.1.0:
🏷️ Channel tags - organise your subscribed channels into tags like #gaming, #music or #news and queue only videos from the channels you want
👁️ Queue Unwatched - skip videos you have already started. QueueTube reads YouTube's own progress bars and filters them out automatically
🔄 Continue Previous Queue - closed your tab halfway through? Return to your subscriptions page and pick up exactly where you left off
🎧 Media key and headphone support - use your keyboard media keys or headphone button to skip to the next video without switching to Chrome. Works in the background while you game, work, or browse elsewhere

Privacy:
QueueTube requests two browser permissions. scripting is required so the extension popup can read and write your channel list and tags in the YouTube tab's local storage. Without this, the tag manager cannot save or retrieve your data. Tabs is required so the popup can identify your open YouTube tab to communicate with it. These permissions are used solely within youtube.com. QueueTube does not access your Google account, your YouTube watch history, or any page outside of youtube.com. No data is ever transmitted to any server. Your active queue is stored in session memory and cleared automatically when you close the tab. Your channel list, tags, and saved queue position persist across sessions in your browser's local storage but never leave your device.
