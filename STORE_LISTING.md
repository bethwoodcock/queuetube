## Short description (up to 132 chars)
Queue your YouTube subscription feed and watch videos back-to-back like a playlist — with autoplay, skip, and progress bars.

## Detailed description
Tired of clicking each video one by one in your YouTube subscriptions?

QueueTube adds a single "Queue Subscriptions" button to your YouTube subscriptions feed. Click it and every video on the page is instantly queued for back-to-back playback — just like a playlist.

**Features:**
• One-click queuing of your entire subscription feed
• Automatically plays the next video when one ends
• Skip forward or back with prev/next buttons
• Click any video in the queue panel to jump straight to it
• Queue panel shows real video titles so you know what's coming next
• Watch progress bars — videos you've partially watched show a red progress bar in the queue panel, just like YouTube's own thumbnails, so you can decide at a glance whether to skip
• Scroll down before queuing to load even more videos (300+ possible!)
• Flexible sidebar browsing — click a suggested video and your queue is saved in the background, ready to return to with one click. Or open it in a new tab to keep your queue playing uninterrupted
• Requires zero browser permissions — no access to your account, history, or any data

**Privacy:**
QueueTube stores your queue in your current tab's session memory only. No data is ever collected, stored permanently, or transmitted anywhere. The queue is gone the moment you close the tab.
